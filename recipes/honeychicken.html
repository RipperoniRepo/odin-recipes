Honey Chicken

Description

Sticky and Easy Honey Garlic Chicken made simple, with the most amazing and addictive 5-ingredient honey garlic sauce that is so good you'll want it on everything! Quick and easy using chicken thighs or breasts, your new family favorite chicken dinner is here! Serves 6.

Ingredients

  • 6 chicken thighs
  • garlic salt and pepper
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 6 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1/3 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth or water
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce

Steps

  1. Season chicken with salt, pepper, and garlic powder; set aside
  2. Heat a pan or skillet over medium high heat; heat a tablespoon of olive oil in the pan then sear chicken thighs on both sides until golden and cooked through, using broth or water in the pan every so often to keep the chicken hydrated.
  3. When chicken is cooked through, add crushed garlic cloves between the chicken and fry until fragrant (about 30 seconds).
  4. Add the 1/3 C honey, 1/4 C broth, 2 TBSP vinegar, and 1 TBSP soy sauce and increase heat to medium-high.
  5. Cook until sauce reduces and thickens slightly (3-4 minutes)
  6. Garnish with parsley and serve over vegetables, rice, pasta, or with a salad.
