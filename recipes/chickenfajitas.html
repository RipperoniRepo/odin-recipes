Chicken Fajitas

Description

Chicken fajitas are an easy and flavorful weeknight meal. This Mexican recipe that everyone will love can be whipped up in less than 30 minutes. Serves 6.

Ingredients

Chicken

  • 3 chicken thighs, sliced into strips
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 bell peppers, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Fajita Seasoning

  • 1/2 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1/2 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper

Steps

  1. Add the fajita seasoning ingredients together in a small bowl and whisk together. Season the chicken thigh strips with it.
  2. Heat the 2 TBSP olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Sear the chicken strips 7-8 minutes on each side until cooked through to at least 165 degrees.
  3. While chicken cooks, cut the bell peppers and onion into thin slices if not done already.
  4. When chicken finishes cooking, remove to a plate and let rest for a couple of minutes.
  5. While chicken rests, add bell pepper and onion slices to same pan used for chicken over medium heat and saute for 7-8 minutes, stirring frequently.
  6. When veggies are done, serve immediately with tortillas, cheese, and any additional toppings you may like.
